## Section Order

1. **Headline Trends** — What's moving today
2. **Sentiment Snapshot** — What people are saying
3. **Deep Dive** — Detailed analysis of top story/trend
4. **Commercial Opportunity** — Where the angle is
5. **Watch List** — What to keep an eye on
6. **Sources** — Links to referenced material

## Style Notes

- British English throughout
- Concise, commercially focused
- No fluff — every sentence should earn its place
- Use bullet points for scannability
- Bold key figures and names
